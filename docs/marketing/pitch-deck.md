## Slide 11: Risks & Mitigations

### Transparency & Risk Management

**Key Risks & Our Mitigation Strategies:**

**1. AI Accuracy Risk**
- **Risk**: AI fails to extract event details accurately
- **Impact**: User frustration, churn, bad reviews
- **Mitigation**:
  - Current 99% accuracy with continuous improvement
  - Human verification option for edge cases
  - User feedback loop to retrain model
  - Fallback to manual editing with smart suggestions

**2. Calendar Integration Fragmentation**
- **Risk**: Calendar APIs change, breaking integrations
- **Impact**: Core functionality fails
- **Mitigation**:
  - Support native device calendars first (most stable)
  - Standard iCal/CalDAV protocols as fallback
  - Partnerships with Google, Apple, Microsoft
  - Regular integration testing & monitoring

**3. User Adoption Friction**
- **Risk**: Users don't understand value proposition quickly
- **Impact**: High download-to-active drop-off
- **Mitigation**:
  - Streamlined onboarding (30 seconds)
  - Interactive tutorial on first launch
  - Sample event capture demonstration
  - A/B testing different onboarding flows

**4. Competition from Big Tech**
- **Risk**: Google Lens adds calendar integration
- **Impact**: Lose differentiation, harder to acquire users
- **Mitigation**:
  - Build network effects & community moat
  - Focus on superior UX & speed
  - B2B relationships with event organizers
  - Potential acquisition target for big tech

**5. Privacy & Data Concerns**
- **Risk**: Users worried about photo data usage
- **Impact**: Trust issues, slow adoption
- **Mitigation**:
  - Privacy-first architecture (local processing)
  - No photo storage without explicit permission
  - GDPR & CCPA compliant from day one
  - Clear, transparent privacy policy

**6. Monetization Challenges**
- **Risk**: Users resist paying for premium
- **Impact**: Revenue lower than projected
- **Mitigation**:
  - Strong free tier to build habit
  - Clear premium value proposition
  - Multiple revenue streams (B2B, API, partnerships)
  - Flexible pricing experiments

**7. Seasonal Demand**
- **Risk**: Event activity varies by season
- **Impact**: Revenue volatility
- **Mitigation**:
  - Geographic diversification (opposite seasons)
  - Non-music event categories (always-on)
  - Annual subscription model smooths revenue
  - B2B contracts provide baseline
